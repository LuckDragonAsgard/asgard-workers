## The pitch in one line
**Enter your student data once. Run every school sport from it.**

---

## The problem every school PE coordinator knows

Each year, every school in Australia re-enters its students into a different spreadsheet for **every single sport**: cross-country, athletics, swimming, footy, netball, soccer. Carnival day means clipboards at the finish line, hand-written times, and a Friday-night transcription session into a results PDF that nobody reads.

District carnivals are worse. Eight schools email each other Excel sheets. The convener pastes them into a master, allocates bibs, prints lane sheets, then chases down half a dozen wrong age groups before the starting gun goes off.

Parents leave the carnival without knowing their kid's time. Coordinators leave with an evening's worth of data entry ahead of them.

## The fix

**School Sport Portal** is one roster, one timing app, one public results page — for every school sport, every season, every year.

| Plain old spreadsheets | School Sport Portal |
|---|---|
| Re-key students per sport | One roster, all sports |
| Hand-time on a stopwatch | Phone-tap timing — Marshal, Lane, Video Finish modes |
| Friday-night transcription | Public results live as each finisher crosses |
| Email Excel sheets between schools | Shared roster across district / division |
| Results PDF on noticeboard | Public results page parents check on their phone |
