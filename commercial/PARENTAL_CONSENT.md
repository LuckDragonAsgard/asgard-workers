# Parental Consent Letter — Sport Portal (template)

**For schools to send home before publishing student names/times publicly.**

Schools can copy this letter onto their letterhead, replace the bracketed fields, and either send it as a paper note in school bags or via the school's regular communication channel (Compass, Sentral, Operoo, Schoolzine).

A short signed-form version follows the letter.

---

## Long version — letter

> [SCHOOL LETTERHEAD]
>
> Dear Parents/Guardians,
>
> **Re: Publication of student names and results in school sport events**
>
> [SCHOOL NAME] has chosen **School Sport Portal** to manage our school sport calendar, race-day timing, and results pages. This brief letter explains what information about your child will be used, how, and how to opt out if you prefer.
>
> ### What information is held about your child
> - Given name
> - Surname
> - Year level
> - House (where applicable)
> - Date of birth (year only — for age-group eligibility)
> - Their times and finishing places in school sporting events
>
> ### What is published publicly
> Where the school chooses to publish a results page (for example our Athletics Carnival results), each student appears as **"first name + surname initial"** by default — for example *"Henry F"*. Full surname appears only on request from the parent or guardian for senior representative results (e.g. district/division/regional teams), and even then only with separate signed consent.
>
> Public results pages do **not** show your child's date of birth, address, parent name, photograph, or any contact detail.
>
> ### Where the data is stored
> All data is stored in Australia (Cloudflare's Sydney region) under a Data Processing Agreement between [SCHOOL NAME] and Luck Dragon Pty Ltd (operator of School Sport Portal). The platform meets the Australian Privacy Principles and is provided under our school's standard third-party privacy framework. A Privacy Impact Assessment has been completed and is available on request.
>
> ### How long it is kept
> Active student records are kept for the duration of your child's enrolment plus the current calendar year. Historical sport results are kept for 7 years (matching school records-retention norms). On withdrawal from the school, all records are deleted on request within 14 days.
>
> ### Your choice
> You may:
>
> - Consent to your child's full name + surname appearing publicly (rare — only if your child represents the school in district/division/regional sport).
> - Consent to your child's first name + surname-initial appearing publicly (the default — recommended).
> - Opt your child out of public publication. Your child will still take part in events and their result will appear on internal school reports, but not on the public page.
>
> Please complete the form below (or via Compass / Sentral) and return by [DATE]. If we don't hear from you we'll apply the default ("first name + surname-initial").
>
> If you have any questions please contact the Sport Coordinator [NAME] on [EMAIL] or the Privacy Officer [NAME] on [EMAIL].
>
> Sincerely,
>
> [PRINCIPAL NAME]
> [SCHOOL NAME]

## Notes for the school

- This template is provided as a starting point. Schools should adapt to their own letterhead, privacy officer contact, and parent-communication norms.
- Where the school's existing enrolment-time privacy collection notice already covers "publication of student name and image in school publications", a separate consent letter is not strictly required for the *first-name + surname-initial* default. Many schools still send this letter for transparency.
- For Catholic and independent schools: please review your diocesan / board privacy framework — some require a fresh consent for any new third-party platform regardless of prior collection-notice scope.
